About PissDonkeys

Described by SPIN magazine as "the sound of Tom Jones being sexually assaulted with a cattle prod inside of a steel port-a-potty", PissDonkeys have made a name for themselves pioneering their unique brand of Country, Latin, Contemporary Christian, and the despairing wails of The Forgotten which they have dubbed "Jizzcore".
